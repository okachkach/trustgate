How It Works

This is how TrustGate provides a secure and reliable transaction for both buyers and sellers, step-by-step.

1. Transaction Agreement

Both the buyer and seller agree on the terms of the sale and a transaction is created on TrustGate. We verify all the details to ensure everyone is aligned.

2. Funds Secured

The buyer sends the agreed-upon payment to our secure platform. We hold the funds in escrow, so the seller knows the money is ready and waiting for them.

3. Product Delivery

With the payment secured, the seller confidently ships the item. They don't have to worry about the buyer disappearing or not paying.

4. Buyer Approval

Once the buyer receives the product and confirms it's as described, they notify TrustGate. This is the green light for us to release the funds.

5. Seller Gets Paid

The money is immediately transferred to the seller, completing the transaction. If there's an issue, our clear dispute process ensures a fair resolution for everyone.
